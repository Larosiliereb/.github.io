Dual Boulder program

My partner and I created this code as our final project. In this game there is a volcano erupting
and you need to watch for falling debris using the left and right arrow key. After round 20, boulders will
start rolling from the right while debris falls from the sky, use spacebar to jump and avoid rolling boulders.
You begin the game with 3 lives and lose one for every hit you take from the boulders. Every 30 points you will
get the chance to pick up another life.
